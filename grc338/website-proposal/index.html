Introduction

Palm of Your Hand

Palm of Your Hand is a small, neighborhood plant shop giving people the means to start their own garden. They offer seed packets and pre-potted plants for customers to start or expand their home gardens. The company encourages a green lifestyle and the incorporation of nature into the living space.

Target audience

The target audience for the products at Palm of Your Hand are people in the age range of 25-65, preferably with a knack for home decorating or garden care. The site would not be catering to any specific gender as anyone can have the desire to garden.

Additionally, people who spend a good deal of time at home would benefit from the products offered in-store. Single individuals looking to fill their time by caring for something that does not need as much attention as a pet would probably like to purchase from Palm of Your Hand as well.

About

We are a group of people with a passion for plants! Often times, people take for granted the natural beauty of the planet and our goal is to spread that love to the community. For close to 10 years we have been a staple of plant care in this neighborhood and hope to continue bringing nature into the home.
